CNC machines can work with a wide variety of materials, including metals, plastics, and composites. Some common examples include aluminum, brass, steel, titanium, and carbon fiber.

CNC machines offer several advantages over manual machines, including higher precision, faster production times, and the ability to produce complex shapes and designs. They also require less manual labor, which can lead to cost savings and increased efficiency.

Our CNC machines use industry-standard software, including CAD/CAM software and machine-specific programming software. We can accept a wide range of file formats, including DXF, DWG, STEP, and IGES.
